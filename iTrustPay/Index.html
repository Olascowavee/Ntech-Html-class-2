Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a deposit with iTrustPay?

+

Making a deposit is simple! Just send the command 'sociallender deposit [fulfiller_phone_no] [amount]' to initiate the process. Our verified fulfiller will meet you to collect the cash, and once confirmed, your account will be credited instantly.

How secure are my transactions?

+

Every transaction is secured with unique transaction codes and real-time verification systems. We use Monnify integration for payment processing and have a trusted network of verified fulfillers to ensure safe cash handling.

How do withdrawals work?

+

To withdraw, use the command 'sociallender withdraw [fulfiller_phone_no] [amount]'. You'll receive payment instructions with a unique transaction code. Meet with the assigned fulfiller to collect your cash, then confirm the withdrawal using the provided code.

Who are fulfillers and how are they verified?

+

Fulfillers are our trusted partners who handle cash transactions. Each fulfiller undergoes strict verification processes and maintains a social reputation score based on their transaction history and user feedback.
